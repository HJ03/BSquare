Centre Manager

Centre Manager shall be responsible for Office Administration and Office Reception”.

Job Profile: Handling Office Accounts/ Customer Accounts/ Maintaining Customer as well as vendor reports/ Handling Customer Queries and resolving within deadlines / Handling Reception for Walk-in of our (mainly Existing) Clients. / Sending Proposals to new clients, etc.

Eligibility: Graduation or Under-graduation but with experience.

Experience: Minimum 6 months, Fresher acceptable if possesses good English Communication Skills.

Location: Ahmedabad, Gujarat


Accounts Manager

Accounts Manager shall be responsible for Group Company’s Accounts Handling & Management in all the stages.

Job Profile: Handling Office Accounts/ Customer Accounts/ Maintaining Customer as well as vendor accounts, TDC Filing, GST Returns Filing, Tally Accounts, Assisting the Statutory Audit of Group Accounts, etc.

Eligibility: Graduation or Under-graduation but with experience. Must Possess Basic Knowledge of Accountancy, Tally, GST, etc.

Experience: Preferably 12 months in Accountancy .

Location: Ahmedabad, Gujarat


Business Development Manager
